Our Dental Services

At 100SMILES Dental Care, we provide a wide range of treatments for all ages. Our friendly and qualified team ensures that every visit is as comfortable and positive as possible.

Annual Dental Check-Up

Our recommended yearly check-up includes a full examination, professional cleaning, and oral hygiene advice to keep your smile healthy and bright.

Preventive Care

Regular check-ups, cleaning, and fluoride treatments help prevent cavities, gum disease, and other oral health issues before they start.

Restorative Treatments

We provide fillings, crowns, bridges, and root canal therapy to restore damaged or decayed teeth to full function and comfort.

Orthodontics

Led by Dr Johanna Grey, our orthodontic treatments include braces and clear aligners to gently move teeth into alignment and improve your smile.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Whitening, veneers, and cosmetic contouring can enhance your natural smile and boost your confidence.

Children's Dentistry

Gentle care for children to create positive dental experiences, including fluoride protection and early monitoring of tooth development.

Implants & Oral Surgery

Dental implants and minor oral surgeries are performed using modern technology and gentle techniques for lasting results.

How often should I get a dental check-up?

Your dental practitioner will recommend a time frame that is appropriate for you based on the state of your oral health. For example, people with very low risk of dental problems may not need to be seen by a dental practitioner as often as a person with complex dental problems. However, it's recommended the time between check-ups is no longer than 2 years.
