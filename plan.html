Goal

The goal of this website is to promote 100SMILES Dental Care and make it easier for patients to find information and book appointments online. It aims to present the clinic's services, introduce the team, and encourage new patients to visit. The website will also provide clear contact details and special offers for first-time visitors.

Success Evaluation

Success will be measured through an increase in online bookings and enquiries, as well as feedback from patients who visit the site. A reduction in the number of phone enquiries for simple questions (e.g., hours) will also show that the site is effective and easy to use and the customer's could directly message the client.

Target Audience

The main audience includes families and individuals in the Razole area looking for a reliable local dentist. The site is designed to be friendly and welcoming, with simple language, large headings, and a calm blue-and-white colour scheme to create a feeling of trust and professionalism. It will appeal to adults booking appointments for themselves or their children.

Site Structure

The website consists of five main pages linked from a consistent navigation bar:

  • Home - Overview of the clinic and welcome message
  • Services - Details of treatments offered and promotions
  • About - Mission, story, and staff introductions
  • Contact - Location, contact form, and contact details
  • Plan - planning structure

Design Features

  • Blue and white theme for a clean, medical feel.
  • Simple fonts and clear structure using sections and divs.
  • Responsive design that adjusts to mobile and desktop screens.
  • Images provided by the client to maintain authenticity.
